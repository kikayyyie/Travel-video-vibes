Quantum Boxes: The Box Model Challenge

Your mission: Precisely calibrate these two "quantum boxes" so they both measure **exactly 250px by 250px** in total visible space (including their internal padding and external border). Observe the fundamental differences in how their dimensions are calculated!

Dimensional Anomaly (Content-Box)

This box's `width` and `height` define its **content area** only. Its padding and border are **added on top**, expanding its overall size. Calculate carefully to hit 250px total!

Synchronized Cube (Border-Box)

This box's `width` and `height` define its **total outer dimensions** (content + padding + border). It's much more intuitive! Just set the total size and add the special property.
